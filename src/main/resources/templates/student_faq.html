How to send request to administrator

  1. Click on button "Request" on navigation bar.

  2. Enter your request title and content into form

  3. Click button "Send Request".

  4. You can track your request progress on table on the left side.

How to book a bed?

  1. Choose "Booking" on the navigation bar.

  2. Choose type of bed.

  3. Select data dorm > floor > room > bed.

  4. Select start date and end date.

  5. Click on button "Book Now" and make Payment.
