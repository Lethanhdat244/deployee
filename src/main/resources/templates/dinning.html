Dining at FPT Dorm

Welcome to the dining services at FPT Dorm! We are committed to providing our residents with a variety of delicious and nutritious meals that cater to different tastes and dietary needs. Our dining facilities are designed to offer a comfortable and inviting atmosphere where students can enjoy their meals, socialize, and relax.

Menu Highlights:

  • Daily Specials: Enjoy a different special meal each day, crafted by our talented chefs.
  • Vegetarian Options: We offer a wide range of vegetarian dishes to ensure everyone has something delicious to eat.
  • International Cuisine: Sample flavors from around the world with our rotating international menu.
  • Healthy Choices: We provide a variety of healthy meal options to support your wellbeing.

Our dining hall is open from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily. We also offer meal plans that are flexible and can be customized to fit your schedule and preferences. Whether you need a quick bite between classes or a leisurely dinner with friends, our dining services have you covered.
